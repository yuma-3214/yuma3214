Frequently Asked Questions

What is sports psychology?

Sports psychology is the study of how psychological factors influence sports, athletic performance, exercise, and physical activity.

How can I improve my mental toughness?

Mental toughness can be improved through consistent practice, goal setting, visualization, and working with a coach or psychologist.
